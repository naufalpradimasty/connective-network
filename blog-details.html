Our Story

Connective is a social networking platform for communities based on a clear problem of fragmentation we saw in the current digital world. Our mission is to connect communities locally, regionally and globally while enabling them to be more productive and successful.

To achieve our mission we provide services to help communities find and connect with other like-minded communities and/or affiliated businesses, promote community interaction by allowing users to release news, exchange ideas, identify opportunities, collaborate and share knowledge, create and host events using public or private settings.
