Our Story

Connective is a social networking platform for communities based on a clear problem of fragmentation we saw in the current digital world. Our mission is to connect communities locally, regionally and globally while enabling them to be more productive and successful.

To achieve our mission we provide services to help communities find and connect with other like-minded communities and/or affiliated businesses, promote community interaction by allowing users to release news, exchange ideas, identify opportunities, collaborate and share knowledge, create and host events using public or private settings.

Affiliation and Ecosystems

One of the most powerful features in Connective is Affiliation (patent pending) - flexibly linking an unlimited number of communities together in an organic eco-system for cross-community communication and expanded audiences. Using affiliation, posts can be shared with affiliated communities without the sender being a member of them and comments are consolidated in one stream across communities, eliminating the silo’s that occur in other group-based systems.

Communicate, Collaborate and Engage
OUR UNIQUE FEATURES
COMMUNICATION
Social Posts
Multiple posting types such as News, Photos and Dialogue. Each type of post can have extensive content and multiple attachments (images, documents, videos), viewed in a horizontal carousel. Posts can be private or public and shared with multiple communities and affiliates at one time. Email notifications can be sent and public posts can also be shared out to social media and delete posts from everywhere!.
Multi-channel Chat
A one stop platform for all your community and network needs from websites to robust communities to mobile apps.
Q&A Forum
Based on popular demand, Connective now includes functionality for all members to ask and answer questions by category (similar to Quora). Available to all members without being in a community. Features include anonymous questions, upvoting, attaching images and documents to questions and answers, viewing most popular questions and answers.
Exchange Knowledge and Profiles
OUR UNIQUE FEATURES
CONTENT
Content Library
Includes a “content management” capability for RSS feeds and user generated articles. Curate and publish articles and videos tagged by topic and author. Content can be easily curated, searched, shared and commented on by readers. Content can also be available to non-members and shared across external social media platforms for encouraging membership.
Expert Profiles
Connective has unique profile features for Experts and Partners to have their own presence on the platform, with their own “profile page” that includes their Expertise, Articles/Media, Chat with an Expert, and most importantly, the ability to book and pay for time with an Expert and to initiate a Zoom session right in the platform!
User Profiles
Connective has flexible and robust profile features for users and includes a personal banner, Overview Information and Social Media Links, Interests, Expertise, Experience and Chat with a member. Member search is also available along with an “online” indicator.
Multiple Member Types
In Connective, the user experience can be unique to different user types. Examples of types includes users, members, experts, partners/sponsors, curators, contributors, nonprofits, businesses, site admins and super admins. Different types have different permissions and user experiences can be unique by category too - for example investors might have different views or features than companies.
Schedule Meetings and Events
OUR UNIQUE FEATURES
EVENTS
Meeting Scheduler
Invite members from multiple communities and external participants to meetings, chats and online sessions. Functionality includes time zones, locations, attachments, a guest list and RSVP email notifications. Meetings will also show on the community calendar.
Online & Offline
For combining online and offline engagement, use Carii’s multi-week functionality to encourage smaller groups to connect online and offline in hosted local gatherings. Carii’s multi-week Agenda dashboard combines relevant content, multiple gatherings and team and member chats for collaboration.
Formal Events
Generate revenue with paid events with registrations, branded event templates can be set up with a banner, location/map, agenda/session selection, speaker bios and multiple categories of registration payments. Importantly, each Event automatically sets up a Event community, so that all members who register are included in that Event community and can interact with each before, during and after the event, elongating the experience.

Generate Revenue for your Organization
OUR UNIQUE FEATURES
REVENUE
Advertising
With multiple capabilities for advertising by community, a demographic can be targeted for specific advertising. Sponsors can have their own presence on the platform, with their own communities that members can join. Provides value for reaching the membership audience by being imbedded in the eco-system for native advertising.
Memberships
Flexibly set-up multiple levels of memberships depending on your model – Free, Single Level, Multi-Level such as Teams, Students, Corporate. Memberships can be monthly or annual and can also be free for a trial period.
Fundraising
Access the best of fundraising tools within the powerful community and communication capabilities of the Connective platform. Manage fundraising campaigns for Nonprofits or Causes – from setup to donations to donor engagement. Includes a list of donors, goal thermometer, teams raising money for a campaign, attaching campaigns to communities and a consolidated display all open campaigns.
Marketplace
Provide affiliate sponsor placement for special benefits for members. Coaches, Experts and Consultants can provide services via an embedded booking system and run Zoom sessions right from the platform. Resellers can be set up to sell products and services in their own eCommerce store. Marketplace is part of the custom solution so contact us for assistance.
Networking, Matching & Engagement Coins
OUR UNIQUE FEATURES
NETWORKING
Networking
Connect with other members, other communities, or even other networks with the flexible technology of Connective. The only platform that provides a ‘network of networks’ connecting across different organizational sites.
Matching/RFP’s
Leverage technology for automated matching for referrals, business to business networking, RFP’s for services, investors to companies and other examples. This functionality can be easily customized to match different elements and will be enhanced to include AI technology as it progresses.
Activity Coins
Connective has powerful functionality for measuring user activity along 19 different metrics (such as Logging in, Sharing, Posting, Commenting....). Users can go up levels by accumulating Coins. Coins settings can be customized by site. As the Coins accumulate, users can donate them to fundraisers, or allocate them to other users, or use them for rebates.
